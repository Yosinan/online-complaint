About Us

Our mission is to transform the existing manual compliant management system into an automate system.

We are here to make complaints easier to coordinate, monitor, track and resolve, and to provide company with an effective tool to identify and target problem areas, monitor complaints handling performance and make business improvements.

The main purpose of this project is to help the public in knowing their place details and getting their problems solved in online without going to the officer regularly until the problem is solved. By this system the public can save his time and eradicate corruption in government offices. Its main purpose is to provide a smart and easy way through web Application for Complaint registration and its Tracking and eradicating Bribing system and thus to prevent Corruption.
